ABOUT

My wife Rachel and I currently live in Lewiston, Idaho with our two dogs. One is a pug, Henry, and the other dog, Ripley, is a miniature schnauzer. My current job is at Vista Outdoors, where I am a millwright in a factory that makes explosives(primers) for bullets. I enjoy my job there because I like the troubleshooting aspect of my work, and I get to play with robots all day. In my spare time I work with my wife on our freelance business, CodePug Creative, and she teaches me how to be better at design.

I like to do many things outside of work. Rachel and I look forward to fishing each spring which I enjoy, despite not being very good at it. We both like to get out and take the dogs on walks or play a round of frisbee golf in the park. I also like to plink around on my guitar or play with my electronic music equipment if I get bored.

I grew up in Lewiston Idaho, but spent my teen years in Belgium. I learned how to speak some Dutch, and I learned how to eat delicious chocolates really well. Living overseas taught me to appreciate other cultures and I hope to visit there again someday.

I decided to pursue web development after I had received my Communications Degree. Computers have always interested me, and I really enjoy learning anything development related. I am a fast learner, which is why I love web development, there is always something new to learn.
